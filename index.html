Kajiado is a town in Kajiado County, Kenya. The town is located 80 km south of Nairobi, along the Nairobi â€“ Arusha highway (A104 road). Kajiado is the headquarters for Kajiado County. The name Kajiado comes from the word Olkeju-ado which means The Long River in the Maasai language. The seasonal river named after the town runs from West to the East of the town. The original name for Kajiado was Olpurapurana, which means a round elevation.
